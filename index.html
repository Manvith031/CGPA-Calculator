Instructions

CIE Marks: Continuous Internal Evaluation, out of 50.

End Sem Marks: End Semester Examination, out of 100 for core subjects, out of 50 for specific subjects like Professional Writing, Scientific Foundation of Health, and Indian Constitution.

Calculation Logic:

  • Total Marks for core subjects = CIE (out of 50) + (End Sem / 2) (converted to 50). This sum is out of 100.
  • Total Marks for Professional Writing, Scientific Foundation of Health, Indian Constitution = CIE (out of 50) + End Sem (out of 50). This sum is out of 100.
  • The total marks (out of 100) are then converted to a 10-point grade score using a predefined scale.
  • SGPA is calculated as (Sum of (Grade Score * Credits)) / Total Credits.

Ensure all marks are entered correctly before calculating.
